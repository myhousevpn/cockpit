Wireguard

WireGuard is an open-source software that helps you create a secure connection to the internet, like a private tunnel that hides your online activity from others. Think of it as a super simple and fast way to keep your online browsing safe and private, much like how a secure lock keeps your home safe.

Download Wireguard
Nextcloud

Nextcloud is an open-source software that lets you create your own online storage space, like your own personal Google Drive or Dropbox. You can store, share, and access your files from anywhere, but the key difference is that you have full control over where your data is stored because it runs on your own server.

Download Nextcloud
